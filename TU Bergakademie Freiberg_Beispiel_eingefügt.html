Our students

After more than a decade into the programme, we experience (with some fluctuation) that our students in the majority are from emerging markets (like China, India, and East Europe), from developed countries (like Germany, France, USA), and from developing countries (like Iran, Nigeria, Nepal).

It is this diversity in culture and educational background which makes this programme particularly inspiring and apt to generate awareness for the particular opportunities and challenges of international business in the emerging and developing world.

These are the most common home countries of our students.
